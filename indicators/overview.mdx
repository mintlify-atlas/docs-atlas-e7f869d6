## Indicator Categories

### Momentum Indicators

**RSI (Relative Strength Index)** measures the speed and magnitude of price movements, helping identify:
- Overbought conditions (above 70)
- Oversold conditions (below 30)
- Divergences signaling potential reversals

### Trend Indicators

**EMA (Exponential Moving Average)** identifies the prevailing trend direction:
- Price above EMA = Bullish trend
- Price below EMA = Bearish trend
- EMA crossovers = Trend changes

### Volatility Indicators

**ATR (Average True Range)** measures market volatility to:
- Calculate appropriate stop-loss distances
- Assess market conditions (high/low volatility)
- Adjust position sizing

### Price Action Indicators

**Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, and Supply/Demand Zones** identify key price levels where:
- Institutional traders have placed large orders
- Price imbalances create trading opportunities
- Support and resistance are likely to hold

<Info>
Price action indicators are particularly powerful in the gold market (XAUUSD) due to high institutional participation.
</Info>

## Supply and Demand Zones

Supply and demand zones are critical price areas where:

**Supply Zones** (Resistance):
- Areas where selling pressure exceeds buying pressure
- Price previously declined sharply from these levels
- Potential reversal or resistance zones

**Demand Zones** (Support):
- Areas where buying pressure exceeds selling pressure
- Price previously rallied sharply from these levels
- Potential reversal or support zones

<Tip>
The AI identifies these zones by analyzing price action patterns and volume characteristics across multiple timeframes.
</Tip>
